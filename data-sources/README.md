We have a preview of what the datasets are likely to look like. The full data sources are in the team4-docker file:
-	sample_parks_10.csv: 10 row sample of the “pybaseball” API.
-	sample_kaggle_parks_10: 10 row sample of the Kaggle Ballparks (MLB Ballparks) dataset.
-	sample_statcast_10.csv: 10 row sample of the Seamheads database.
-	sample_weather_10.csv: 10 row sample of the Openmeteo weather API
